cron

0 * * 5... WTF

      0 5 * * 1-5 ant

      # Minutes Hours Days Months DayOfWeek Command
      0 = minute (0-59)
      5 = hour (0-23)
      * = day of the month (1-31)
      * = month of the year (1-12)
      1-5 = day of the week (0-6 with 0=Sunday)
