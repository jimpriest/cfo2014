Three R's

  • Learning new things is hard!!!!!!
  • IDE switch (ColdFusion Builder anyone????), new programming languages, etc. are all things we struggle to learn as developers
  • Training ourselves take a lot of work…
  • Repetition – it’s not fun but we know if we do things over and over again it will be drilled into our brains
  • Resolution – stick with it! Sometimes learning new techniques is difficult! But the payouts at the and will be rewarding!
  • Re-evaluate – constantly reevaluate your tools and techniques. There is always something new out there!

cron

0 * * 5... WTF

      0 5 * * 1-5 ant -f C:\ant\db.build.xml copy-prd-to-local

      # Minutes Hours Days Months DayOfWeek Command
      0 = minute (0-59)
      5 = hour (0-23)
      * = day of the month (1-31)
      * = month of the year (1-12)
      1-5 = day of the week (0-6 with 0=Sunday)
